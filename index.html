Support for Aidan, Clay, & Holden After House Fire

Team fundraiser
Lauren Fitzmeyer and 2 others are organizing this fundraiser.
Donation protected
Aidan Cowan, Clay Rowland, and Holden Lewis—seniors at Georgia College & State University and members of Kappa Sigma fraternity—lost their home and all of their belongings in a devastating house fire last night. Thankfully, them and the two dogs, Rocky and Milo, made it out safely, but everything else was destroyed.

We’re raising funds to help them replace essentials and recover what they can as they start from scratch. Any support means the world to them during this difficult time. Thank you !
